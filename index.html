Our Goals

At First Steps Developmental Center, we believe strong foundations should start early.

We practice physical activity in all areas and teach healthy, polite eating habits.

While education is important, life is not all about what you learn. Well-rounded, healthy children also develop physically, educationally, and emotionally.
